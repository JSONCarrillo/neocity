Welcome! Enjoy Your Stay!

My name is J, just J. I'm a tech and gaming nerd with a bit of an obsession with the original Bungie-era Halo Games. I started this site because I basically quit social media altogether, save for discord, and was getting really tired of the overly-corporatization and homogenization of the internet. Neocities scratched that itch for the creative outlet I was looking for that hasn't been seen since myspace or early youtube.

Best viewed on desktop. Be sure to enable Javascript!
